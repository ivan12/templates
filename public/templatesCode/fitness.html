Interrogation Room

Do you write meal plans?
I provide macros and a framework. Meal plans fail because they are restrictive. I teach you how to eat for your goals so you can sustain it forever.
Gym or Home?
Either works, but you need resistance. Dumbbells and a bench are the minimum for home. A full gym is preferred for maximum hypertrophy.
What if I have injuries?
We work around them. Movement is often the cure. I'll modify exercises to ensure you train pain-free while rehabbing.
Refund Policy?
If you do the work and don't see results, I'll work with you for free until you do. If you don't do the work, that's on you.
Is this too hardcore for me?
Intensity is relative. The system adapts to your level — effort is non-negotiable.
Is this reckless or controlled?
Controlled. The brand is intense — the method is precise.
  • Evidence-based programming built around proven principles, not ego lifts.
  • Injury-aware modifications to work around limitations while keeping progress moving.
  • Data-driven progression so intensity increases when your performance says you're ready.
