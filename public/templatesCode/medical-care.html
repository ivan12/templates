Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results?
Most patients lose weight within the first 4 weeks. Clinical studies show a weight loss of approx. 15% within a year when combined with lifestyle changes.
Is the medication safe?
GLP-1 analogues have been used for over 10 years. Like any medication, side effects can occur (e.g., nausea at the beginning), but these usually subside quickly. Our doctors will explain everything in detail.
Does health insurance cover it?
Basic insurance usually only covers the costs under certain criteria (BMI > 35 or > 28 with co-morbidities). We help you clarify this with your insurance provider.
